Scarlet Blake, who killed a cat and put animal in blender, found guilty of man's murder

After watching a documentary, a 25 year old named Scarlet Blake, later on decided to live stream and dissect cats. Following this, a woman murdered a man four months after she live streamed dissecting a kitten. She was encouraged to do such a crime by his desire to impress his girlfriend or sexual motivation, but also by watching horrific crimes to kill a cat. After all this, the court decided to sentence him for 24 years.
