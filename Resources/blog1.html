Poe urges passage of revised Animal Welfare Act

On march 20, 2024 ” MANILA – Senator Grace Poe on Wednesday renewed her call for the passage of Senate Bill (SB) No. 2458, which seeks to improve animal welfare in the country, following the brutal killing of a golden retriever named "Killua" in Bato, Camarines Sur which was widely condemned by netizens.”

After the news broke about the dog being killed, namely Killua. Sen. Grace Poe expressed her feelings “heart-broken and enraged”. She warned the public about the Animal Welfare Act of 1998 in which was signed by the Committee on Animal Welfare upon hearing about this news. The Animal Welfare Act of 1998 protects the rights of the animals, prohibits abuse and torture of animals, fails to provide sufficient care or shelter, and forbids animals to use for experiments, or to put any animals in.
